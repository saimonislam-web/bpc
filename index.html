About

The BSMRU Computer Club is a community of tech enthusiasts at Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman University, Kishoreganj. The club aims to promote computer science and technology among students and faculty members. It provides a platform for members to share their knowledge, learn new skills, and participate in various tech-related events. Joining the club is an excellent opportunity to network with like-minded individuals and stay up-to-date with the latest trends in the field of computer science.
